WHAT IS THIS?

This is a revolutonary kit that will help you become the most famous guy on your block.

It was created in 2018 to satisfy the ambition to have a portable and easy to build assistant for the everyday tasks.

In a world more connected in every year that passes, it is important to have someone to be hommie whenever you need the most.

So, for that, we present you the Friend Kit.

HOW IT WORKS?

The package is composed by our brilliant Raspberry Pi hardware and PiSO, yes, PiSO. Plus all the sound harware you need and cables, etc.

PiSO is the ultimate Software that helps you deal with your new assistant only using your voice. He or she will become a friend of yours thanks to PiSO!

All you have to do is follow the instructions, assemble your Friend, turn it on and make a few adjustments to be at your taste.

Finally, you can start using your new Friend to help you schedule appointments, check the best route before you leave home
or even give you tips or answer your real life questions.
